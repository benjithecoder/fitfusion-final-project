LATERAL RAISES

Dumbbells

Target Muscle

LATERAL DELTOIDS

Lateral Raises are crucial for developing broad shoulders. This isolation exercise specifically targets the lateral head of the deltoid, helping to create a wider upper body appearance.

Primary

Lateral Deltoids

Secondary

Trapezius

  1. 1

    Stand with dumbbells at your sides.

  2. 2

    Keep a slight bend in your elbows.

  3. 3

    Raise arms out to sides until shoulder height.

  4. 4

    Pause briefly at the top.

  5. 5

    Lower weights back down slowly.
