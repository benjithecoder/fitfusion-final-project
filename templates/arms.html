BICEPS CURLS

Dumbbells

Target Muscle

BICEPS BRACHII

Bicep Curls are an isolation exercise that targets the biceps brachii. Essential for building arm size and strength, they can be performed with dumbbells, barbells, or cables.

Pimary

Biceps Brachii

Secondary

Brachialis, Forearms

  1. 1

    Stand tall holding dumbbells at sides.

  2. 2

    Keep elbows close to your torso.

  3. 3

    Curl the weights up towards shoulders.

  4. 4

    Squeeze biceps at the top of movement.

  5. 5

    Lower weights slowly with control.
