TRICEPS PUSHDOWN

Cable Machine

Target Muscle

TRICEPS BRACHII

Triceps Pushdowns are one of the most effective exercises for isolating the triceps. Since the triceps make up two-thirds of the upper arm, this exercise is key for arm size.

Primary

Triceps Brachii

Secondary

Anconeus

  1. 1

    Attach rope or bar to high pulley.

  2. 2

    Keep elbows pinned to your sides.

  3. 3

    Push down until arms are fully extended.

  4. 4

    Squeeze triceps hard at the bottom.

  5. 5

    Return slowly to starting position.
