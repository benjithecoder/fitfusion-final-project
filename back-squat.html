BACK SQUAT

Barbell

Target Muscle

QUADRICEPS

The Barbell Back Squat is a compound exercise that primarily targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings. It is often referred to as the "king of all exercises" due to its ability to build lower body strength and power.

Primary

Quadriceps, Glutes

Secondary

Hamstrings, Core

  1. 1

    Position bar on your traps.

  2. 2

    Step back and set feet shoulder-width.

  3. 3

    Lower into a squat, keeping chest up.

  4. 4

    Drive through heels and stand tall
