BENCH PRESS

Barbell

Target Muscle

PECTORALS

The Bench Press is a classic compound exercise for building upper body strength and muscle mass. It primarily works the chest muscles, anterior deltoids, and triceps.

Primary

Pectorals

Secondary

Triceps, Front Delts

  1. 1

    Lie back on bench, eyes under the bar.

  2. 2

    Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders.

  3. 3

    Unrack and lower bar to mid-chest.

  4. 4

    Press bar up until arms are extended.

  5. 5

    Keep feet planted and core tight throughout.
